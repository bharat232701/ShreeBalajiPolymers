About Us

Shree Balaji Polymers is a trusted manufacturer of PP raffia lumps and grinding materials, based in Kundli, Haryana. We specialize in producing high-quality recycled polymer products that meet the needs of various industrial applications like vegetable & fruit crates, pallets, etc.

With a strong focus on quality, consistency, and timely delivery, we have built a reputation as a reliable partner for businesses looking for cost-effective and durable raw materials. Our manufacturing process ensures proper sorting, cleaning, and processing to deliver superior-grade products.
